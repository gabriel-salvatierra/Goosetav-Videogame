# Goosetav-Videogame

3D Unity videogame made in the scope of Da Vinci School Q3

A variety of geese power ups in a linear and balanced platformer with boss battles and 2 extra mini games!

### How to download and play

1. Click `Game` folder
2. Click and Download each `Goosetav-Videogame.partXX.rar`
3. With all downloaded in the same folder just unzip de first part
5. In unzipped folder doubleclick `Goosetav-El-Ganso.exe`
6. Enjoy!
